Our History

Trusted Since 2005

M/S. Sotota Machineries Store was established at Station Road, Jamalpur, Mymensingh Division — the commercial heart of Jamalpur. For over 20 years we have been the go-to destination for agricultural machinery, home accessories, and plumbing fittings.

Our mission: bring top-quality products at honest prices to the farmers and households of Jamalpur. Today we stock 500+ products from 18+ trusted global brands, serving Jamalpur Sadar, Islampur, Sarishabari, Dewanganj, Melandaha, and neighboring districts including Tangail, Netrokona, Sherpur, and beyond.

  • Original products from 18+ reputed international brands
  • Live video call demos — see products before you buy
  • Home delivery across Mymensingh Division
  • Flexible pricing — negotiate via WhatsApp or phone
  • Friendly, knowledgeable staff in Bangla & English
  • Conditional returns — handled fairly and quickly
